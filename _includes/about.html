About

Barnaby Smith is a freelance media composer based in the UK. His work includes production music, branded content, documentary TV and short films. One client says "He has consistently composed and performed extremely good music for our films”. Another, "Excellent work, I'd definitely use him again!”.

Credits include soundtracks to several installations by Double Take Projections, most notably Historic England's Where Light Falls project, and Grindelwald Productions' series of travel films Footloose In Europe.

Barnaby is also an experienced sound designer, audio/dialogue editor and post production mixer. He can fulfil all your audio needs to whatever specification you require.
