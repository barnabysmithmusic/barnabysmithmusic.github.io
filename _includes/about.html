About

Barnaby Smith is a freelance media composer based in the UK. His work includes production music, branded content, documentary TV and short films. One client says "He has consistently composed and performed extremely good music for our films”. Another, "Excellent work, I'd definitely use him again!”.

Most recently he has composed soundtracks to several installations by Double Take Projections, including one at St Paul's Cathedral in London as part of Historic England's Where Light Falls project. Credits include the TV series Footloose In Europe and Greenpeace UK’s anti-fracking protest, The Frack Free Bake Off featuring Emma and Sophie Thompson.

Barnaby was born and raised in Cheshire, and now lives and works in the North East. He specialised in music composition at the University of Sheffield, graduating with a BMus (Hons) degree, and then went on to complete a diploma in Music for the Media with Thinkspace.
