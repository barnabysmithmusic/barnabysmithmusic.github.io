About

  • 1995-1998

    University

    BMus (Hons) Studied music at University of Sheffield under George Nicholson and Adrian Moore.

  • 2004

    My first composition job

    Completed a Diploma in Music for the Media (Thinkspace) with my first job, composing for Grindelwald Productions' "Along The West Highland Way". I have since written and produced music for 20 of their DVD titles, a lot of which have been turned into TV series "Footloose in Europe" which has been broadcast worldwide.

  • 2010

    Short films

    My first short film was Daniel Chadwick's "March 29th", a 10 minute horror film produced in his final year at MMU. I've since gone on to score several short films in a variety of genres (see credits).

  • September 2013

    Studio upgrade

    New equipment brought my studio bang up to date, with a UA Apollo Quad Thunderbolt interface, Pro Tools 11, a new MacBook Pro and a range of the latest virtual instruments from NI and EWQL.
